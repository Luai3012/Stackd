Common questions

Everything you need to know before signing up.

Is Stackd really free to start? +
Yes — no credit card required. The Free plan lets you log up to 5 deals per month with full gap detection and quota tracking. You'll see exactly what Stackd does before spending a dollar.
Can my employer see my Stackd data? +
No. Stackd is a personal app. Your data is stored securely and only accessible to your account. We use row-level security so no other user — or employer — can ever access your records. We never share user data with third parties.
What is the refund policy? +
7-day money back guarantee on all paid plans. Email support@wearestackd.net within 7 days of subscribing and we'll refund you in full — no questions asked.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes — no long-term contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel from your settings at any time. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period, then drop to the Free plan.
Does Stackd integrate with my CRM? +
No integration needed — and that's intentional. Stackd is your personal record, completely separate from your company's systems. You don't need IT approval, CRM access, or admin permissions. You log your own deals in 30 seconds.
What does the AI actually do? +
Stackd uses Claude (by Anthropic) to generate professional documents based on your actual deal data. This includes dispute emails, raise requests, performance reports, and — on the Career plan — interview coaching, STAR answers, LinkedIn rewrites, and salary negotiation scripts. The AI reads your real numbers and writes content specific to your situation, not a generic template.
What's the difference between Pro and Career? +
Pro is everything you need to track commissions and fight for what you're owed. Career adds a full coaching suite — interview prep, STAR answers, LinkedIn audits, and negotiation scripts — for reps who are also building toward their next move. Career is $10/month more than Pro.
