Refund Policy

Last updated: June 2026 · Stackd (wearestackd.net)

7-day money back guarantee

If you are not satisfied with your Stackd subscription within the first 7 days of purchase, email us at support@wearestackd.net and we will issue a full refund — no questions asked.

Who this applies to

This policy applies to all paid Stackd plans — Pro ($19/month) and Career ($29/month) — purchased through our payment provider Paddle.

How to request a refund

Email support@wearestackd.net within 7 days of your initial payment with the subject line "Refund Request". Include the email address associated with your account. We will process your refund within 3-5 business days. Refunds are returned to the original payment method.

After 7 days

After the 7-day guarantee period, subscriptions are non-refundable. However, you can cancel at any time from your account settings and you will not be charged again. You will retain access to your paid plan until the end of your current billing period.

Exceptions

We reserve the right to refuse refunds in cases of abuse — for example, if a user requests a refund, receives it, and then signs up again to exploit the guarantee multiple times. In such cases we may deny the refund request and/or suspend the account.

Cancellations

You can cancel your subscription at any time from Settings → Subscription inside the Stackd app. Cancellation stops future billing immediately. Your subscription remains active until the end of the paid period.

Payment processor

All payments are processed by Paddle, who acts as the Merchant of Record. For payment-related queries you may also contact Paddle directly at paddle.com/support.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email support@wearestackd.net and we'll respond within 24 hours.

Need a refund?

Email us within 7 days and we'll sort it out immediately.
