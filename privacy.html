Privacy policy

Last updated: June 2026

Stackd ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

What we collect

When you use Stackd, we collect your name and email address, deal data you enter manually, your compensation plan details, and basic usage data. We do not collect any data from your employer's systems.

How we use your data

We use your data exclusively to provide the Stackd service. We do not sell your data or share it with your employer or any third party for commercial purposes.

Data security

Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit. Row-level security ensures your data is only accessible to your authenticated account.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete all data we hold about you. You can export your data at any time using the CSV export feature. To request deletion, email support@wearestackd.net.

Cookies

We use only essential cookies required for authentication and session management. No advertising or tracking cookies are used.

Third-party services

Stackd uses Supabase (database and auth), Paddle (payments), and Anthropic (AI features). Each has their own privacy policy governing their use of your data.

Contact

Questions? Email support@wearestackd.net.
