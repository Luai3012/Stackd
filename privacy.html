Privacy policy

Last updated: June 2026 · Stackd (wearestackd.net)

Stackd ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, how long we keep it, and your rights. By using the Service you agree to the practices described here.

1. Data we collect

Data typeWhat it includesWhy we collect it
Account dataEmail address, name, password (hashed)To create and manage your account
Deal & commission dataDeal names, values, commission rates, gap amounts — all entered manually by youTo power the core tracking features
Compensation plan dataQuota targets, commission tiers, plan details you enterTo calculate attainment and gaps
Usage dataPages visited, features used, login timestamps, browser type, IP addressTo improve the Service and diagnose issues

We do not collect any data from your employer's systems. We do not access your work email, CRM, or any external platform.

2. How we use your data

We use your data exclusively to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Stackd Service
  • Send transactional emails (account confirmation, password reset, billing receipts)
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with your employer, with advertisers, or with any third party for commercial purposes.

3. AI features and data handling

When you use AI features (dispute emails, raise requests, STAR answers, etc.), your input is sent to Anthropic's API to generate a response. This means Anthropic processes the text you submit as a prompt.

We use Anthropic's API under their standard commercial terms. Anthropic states that API inputs and outputs are not used to train their models by default. However, we recommend you do not include sensitive personal identifiers (national ID numbers, passport details, bank account numbers) in your AI prompts. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy for full details.

5. Data security

Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure with encryption at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS). Row-level security (RLS) policies ensure your data is only accessible to your authenticated account — no other user can access your deals or compensation data. We do not store plain-text passwords.

While we take security seriously, no system is perfectly secure. In the event of a data breach that affects your personal data, we will notify you by email within 72 hours of becoming aware of it.

6. Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data and deal records within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law (for example, billing records which we keep for 7 years for tax purposes). Anonymised, aggregated usage statistics may be retained indefinitely as they cannot identify you.

7. Cookies

We use only essential cookies required for authentication and session management. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics cookies. A cookie consent notice is shown on first visit — you can dismiss it to accept essential cookies only.

8. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Export — download your deal data at any time via the CSV export feature in the app
  • Objection — object to certain processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, email support@wearestackd.net. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Children

The Service is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18. If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a minor, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. For material changes, we will notify you by email.

11. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Email support@wearestackd.net and we'll respond within 2 business days.
