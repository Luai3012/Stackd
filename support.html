Support

Find answers to common questions or reach out to us directly.

Getting started
How do I create an account?
Go to wearestackd.net/app, click "Sign up", enter your name, email, and a password. You'll be signed in immediately and taken straight to your dashboard.
What is a comp plan and why do I need one?
Your comp plan stores your commission rate and quota target. Stackd uses these to automatically calculate what you should be earning on every deal you log. You only need to set it up once.
Can I use Stackd alongside my company's CRM?
Absolutely — that's exactly how it's designed. Stackd is your personal record, completely separate from your company's systems. No IT approvals, no integrations, no access issues.
Deals and commissions
How does Stackd calculate my expected commission?
Simple: deal value × your commission rate. If you close a $20,000 deal at 8%, Stackd records your expected commission as $1,600 — calculated automatically the moment you log the deal.
What does "disputed" status mean?
When you mark a deal as disputed, Stackd surfaces it in your alerts and gives you the option to draft a professional dispute message to send to your manager.
How do I export my deals?
Click "Export CSV" on your dashboard. This downloads a spreadsheet of all your deals including expected commission, amount received, and gaps.
Privacy and security
Can my employer see my Stackd data?
No. Stackd is a personal app and your data is completely private to your account. We never share user data with employers or third parties. Row-level security ensures no other user can access your records.
How do I reset my password?
On the sign-in screen, click "Forgot your password?", enter your email, and we'll send you a reset link.
Billing
Is Stackd free to start?
Yes. The Free plan lets you log up to 5 deals per month with full gap detection and quota tracking — no credit card required.
What is the refund policy?
We offer a 7-day money back guarantee on all paid plans. Email support@wearestackd.net within 7 days and we'll refund you in full — no questions asked.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes — no long-term contracts. Cancel at any time from your settings. You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period.
What happens to my data if I cancel?
Your data stays in your account until you actively delete it. Cancelling downgrades you to the Free plan — your deals and history remain accessible.

Still have a question?

We respond to every message personally — usually within 24 hours.
